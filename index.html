Uniswap

Uniswap
Type Decentralized exchange
Industry Cryptocurrency
Founded November 2, 2018
Founder Hayden Adams

Uniswap is a decentralized cryptocurrency exchange that uses a set of smart contracts running on the Ethereum blockchain to perform trades. It allows users to trade cryptocurrencies without the need for a centralized intermediary, maintaining the ownership of their assets while trading.

Uniswap is also known for pioneering the automated market maker (AMM) model, where liquidity pools are used to enable trading without the need for an order book.

History

The Uniswap protocol was created by Hayden Adams in November 2018. It was inspired by Ethereum co-founder Vitalik Buterin's idea of creating decentralized exchanges.

Features

Uniswap allows users to swap between various Ethereum-based tokens, and it also provides liquidity pools where users can earn fees by supplying tokens.

Technology

Uniswap operates on the Ethereum blockchain, and its smart contracts are open-source and can be accessed by anyone. The protocol relies on liquidity providers who deposit pairs of tokens into liquidity pools.
